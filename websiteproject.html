WEBSITE PORTFOLIO

Steak-In-It

Steak-In-It is a website for a family-owned cheesesteak food truck based in Fairfield County, Connecticut. Founded in 2023 by Armando, Jackie, and Devin, the truck serves high-quality, freshly made cheesesteaks, combining over 10 years of food truck industry experience.

My role in this project was to create the website for the food truck. I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create a responsive website that showcases the food truck's menu, location, and contact information.

The website features:

  • Welcome Section: An introduction to the brand's mission and history.
  • About Us: The story behind the creation of Steak-In-It, emphasizing their commitment to quality, research, and customer experience.
  • Menu: Showcases their signature cheesesteaks and sides with a visual menu.
  • How It's Done: A detailed description of the cheesesteak-making process, highlighting fresh ingredients, skill, and customization options.
  • Events: Information on catering services for private and corporate events, plus a form to request bookings.
  • Contact Us: Easy access to phone, email, and social media links.

QuickBite - Food Delivery App

WFor a school project, I created QuickBite, a food delivery application designed to make ordering from local restaurants fast, easy, and efficient. Users can browse nearby restaurants, view menus, place orders, and track deliveries in real-time. The app features user authentication, order history, and restaurant recommendations. This project focused on building a robust API using Express.js, MongoDB, and JWT authentication to ensure a smooth and secure user experience.
