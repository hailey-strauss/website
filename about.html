About Me

Hailey Strauss is a dynamic creative professional who recently graduated in May 2023 from High Point University with a Bachelor’s Degree in Social Media and Digital Communications. Alongside her academic journey, she ventured into the world of coding, completing a rigorous Full Stack Flex coding bootcamp in November 2023, expanding her technical expertise and problem-solving skills. Currently, she is preparing to embark on a Masters program at Touro University, pursuing an M.S. Degree in Web and Application Development.

Hailey established herself as a photographer, videographer, and social media specialist. Her knack for visual storytelling led her to capture the essence of various events at High Point University, showcasing them across the institution's digital platforms and marketing materials.She has also served as a marketing intern for Goodroot, a pharmaceutical formulary company, working with the marketing team to design, produce and edit videos of the company’s leadership team members’ critical thought processes which were displayed on the company’s social media accounts. She has carefully produced videos and created graphics while interning for several non-profit organizations and she worked as an art specialist with young children. Hailey also directed, edited and acted in a full-length film

Hailey’s leadership and volunteer community activities include: High Point Universtiy Leadership Fellows, High Point University softball team player and team photographer, University Choir, and WishMakers. Hailey has completed over 300 hours of volunteer work for non-profit organizations which provide services to children, families and elders in community.

With a rich tapestry of experiences in photography, videography, social media, and now coding, Hailey’s journey is marked by a passion for storytelling and a relentless drive to merge creativity with technology to make an impact.
