About Me

I am a skilled developer working toward an M.S. in Web and Application Development with Touro University, NYC. I'm also a Coding Coach with The Coder School where I have the incredible opportunity to combine my passions for coding and teaching by fostering creativity and problem-solving abilities in children and teens. I earned a certificate in Full-Stack Coding via the University of CT and a B.A. in Social Media and Digital Communications at High Point University. My proficiency in web technologies includes: HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, Python as well a many other languages.

As a student photographer at High Point University I specialized in capturing the vibrant moments and stories of the university community which were utilized in the university's marketing materials and website. I have a strong foundation in graphic design, video production, and editing and had the privilege of gaining professional experience as a marketing intern at Goodroot, a health tech company, as well as a social media marketing intern at the National STEM honor society. During these internships, I conducted market research, crafted thought-provoking video content, designed graphics for various social media platforms, and implemented effective email marketing strategies to boost engagement and awareness.

My mission is to utilize my skills and creative talents to develop innovative solutions that have a positive impact on society and the environment. I hold a deep appreciation for collaboration, diversity, and lifelong learning.
